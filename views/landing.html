Welcome to Lander's Ananda Yoga

Ananda is the Sanskrit word for bliss. It is a blissful, peaceful happiness. It is the kind of happiness that does not depend on your circumstances to feel a state of happiness and contentment.

We, at Ananda Yoga Lander, strive to bring competent yoga instruction, compassion, and awareness to our community to the best of our abilities in a form true to each of our own natures in the hope of bringing further awareness to our own inner bliss that we each already possess.
